Adding without Arithmetic operators

Just a few lines of code, Easy right!
But what on earth is going on here

Lets perform addition on number 1 and 2

The binary representation of 1 and 2 are 0001 and 0010

Peforming an AND operation will give us the number 0000, means there is no carry

Now we will perform a OR operation to get 0011

Since there are only 0's in the carry, no carry means the "addition" has completed.

An easy way to approach this question is to think of & AND operation as a way to find the carry , which helps to determine how many iterations should be performed, because after each iteration, the carry will be shifted left by 1 until we are left with 0's.
^ XOR helps us to get the sum of that iteration, we should not worry about the carry because it has been stored and taken cared of from the AND operaiton performed earlier.
